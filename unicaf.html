Project Overview

The Opportunity

From 2021 to 2023, I served as UNICAF's local representative in Botswana, progressing from Service Consultant to Marketing Consultant, and ultimately to Marketing and Business Development Manager. My mission was to establish on-the-ground presence for this Cyprus-based online education provider and build trust within the Batswana community.

UNICAF needed more than digital marketing‚Äîthey needed a local face, authentic community relationships, and strategic partnerships to overcome skepticism about online education and international credentials. I became that bridge, transforming abstract brand awareness into tangible community impact.
