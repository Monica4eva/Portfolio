The Opportunity

In July 2024, I was appointed as Chief Marketing Officer for B Agro Crop Ltd, a women-led agricultural processing company in Ziope, Ghana. The company specialized in processing locally grown vegetables (chili, ginger, okra, garden eggs, habanero peppers) into shelf-stable powdered products to reduce post-harvest losses and empower women farmers.

Over 16 months, I revived their brand presence, created comprehensive marketing systems, represented the company internationally, and achieved exponential growth metrics that positioned B Agro Crop as an emerging leader in sustainable agriculture.

My Mission

Build LinkedIn presence and engagement, develop investor pitch materials, execute Business Shower fundraising campaign, represent company in ESEA Acceleration Programme, conduct Botswana market research for Protifai expansion, and establish sustainable marketing systems.

Timeline & Scope

16 months (July 2024 - November 2025) of strategic marketing leadership. Delivered comprehensive CMO services including brand development, content strategy, investor relations, and market research for international expansion.

The Company Vision

B Agro Crop Ltd was founded with a powerful mission: to reduce post-harvest losses in rural Ghana while empowering women and youth farmers through sustainable agricultural practices. The company transforms fresh vegetables into shelf-stable powdered products, creating economic opportunities and preserving nutritional value.

What This Case Study Demonstrates

This success story showcases my ability to shift stagnant, or slow growth brands, develop comprehensive marketing strategies, create investor-ready materials, conduct international market research, and deliver measurable results that position companies for sustainable growth and impact.

The Challenge

Reviving Engagement

Company had very slow LinkedIn presence, no social media strategy, no brand voice, and no marketing materials. The opportunity was to build a professional brand from absolutely low to no engagement to attract investors and corporate partnerships, a near blank canvas for transformation.

Strategic Fundraising

Company needed to raise GHS 50,000 ($4,500 USD) to complete factory setup and scale operations. This required developing a comprehensive Business Shower fundraising campaign with compelling storytelling and clear calls-to-action.

Accelerator Excellence

Company was accepted into the prestigious ESEA Acceleration Programme, requiring comprehensive pitch decks, financial projections, origin stories, and regular deliverables. This was an opportunity to showcase the company's potential on an international stage.

International Expansion

Protifai (sister company) needed comprehensive Botswana market research for meat delivery app expansion. This required understanding BMC operations, local butcheries, distribution channels, regulatory requirements, and competitive landscapeâ€”an exciting opportunity to shape expansion strategy.

Creating Social Proof

Company needed documented testimonials, success stories, and visual proof of operations to build credibility and trust for fundraising. This was an opportunity to craft compelling narratives that would resonate with investors and partners.

The Opportunity

Success meant positioning B Agro Crop for sustainable growth, investor funding, and international expansion. Every challenge was an opportunity to demonstrate creative problem-solving, strategic thinking, and execution excellence in building a purpose-driven brand.
